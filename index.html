How to use your portal
Everything you need to stay in the loop with your Business Smoothie team, in one place.
Signing in
No password to remember. Enter your email on the login screen and we'll send you a secure sign-in link. Tap it from your inbox and you're in. The link works once and expires after 24 hours — just request a new one anytime.
Projects
Your Projects tab shows what your team is working on for you and where each piece stands. Open a project to see its progress and the tasks inside it.
Tasks
The Tasks tab lists the to-dos tied to your projects. You can see what's done, what's in progress, and what's coming up next.
Onboarding
When you first come on board, the Onboarding tab walks you through the setup steps. Check off the items on your side as you complete them — your team sees your progress and handles their part.
Notes
The Notes tab is a shared space between you and your team. Drop links, questions, or anything you want them to see — they're working from the same notes you are.
Need a hand?
Reach out to your Business Smoothie team anytime — we're here to help you get the most out of your platform.
